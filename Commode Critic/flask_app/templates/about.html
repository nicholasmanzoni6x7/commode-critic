What the App Does

Allows users to share reviews of restrooms they encounter.

Why It's Important

It's important to be able to find your nearest restroom as well as know what to expect.

Sanitation Facts

US has only 8 public restrooms per 100 people, which is very low world-wide. With the advent of the COVID-19, this number has dropped. Sanitation is the 17th leading cause of death worldwide.

Who We Are

We are a team dedicated to raising the issue of sanitation from something taboo to something that must be addressed.
